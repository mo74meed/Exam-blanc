🎯 Diagnostic Key Concepts

Embryology

Hernias: Congenital umbilical hernias occur due to failure of bowel to return to abdominal cavity after physiological herniation; covered by skin. Omphalocele involves herniation into umbilical cord (covered by sac).

High Yield

Wilson's Disease: Autosomal recessive mutation in ATP7B copper transporter. Characterized by Kayser-Fleischer rings, liver disease, neurological disorders, and low ceruloplasmin.

Physiology

Bile & Bilirubin: Conjugated bilirubin is water soluble and excreted in bile. Unconjugated bilirubin is lipid soluble, bound to albumin, and cannot be excreted in urine.

Local Videos Setup Guide

To configure your video files for seamless native playback, follow these instructions:

Step 1: Locate your downloaded videos from the Telegram channel.
Step 2: Copy the files to the **`raw_downloads`** folder inside the **`Exam-blanc`** directory.
Step 3: Run the Sorter renamer script **`python sort_local_videos.py`**. The script will automatically structure your files under the **`videos/`** folder.
Step 4: Reload the web app. The video player will auto-detect the files and play them natively.
